<h3><u>Patch Notes - 08/09/2021</u></h3>

<h3><b><u>MISSION IMPROVEMENTS</u></b></h3>
<p>- Corrected some script issues that would permanently disable saber locking while in gameplay across all missions</p>

<h3><b><i><u>DUEL OF THE FATES (OBI-WAN)</u></i></b></h3>
<p>- Fixed an issue where the player would turn into a Stormtrooper if they were too slow to strike Maul.
<br>- You will no longer be able to move while conversing with Qui-Gon at the end of the mission.</p>

<h3><b><u>DUEL OF THE FATES (QUI-GON JINN)</u></b></h3>
<p>- Added an animation to Qui-Gon during the final stage of the Clone Ambush.
<br>- Removed a faulty Qui-gon saber model during the initial mission that should never have been there in the first place.
<br>- Improved Palpatine's "small use for you" line during the dark side ending.</p>

<h3><b><u>CODE CHANGES</u></b></h3>
<p>- Created a new cvar "g_newforcepowers" to enable or disable npcs using the new force powers
<br>- Created a new cvar "g_allowforceabsorb" to enable or disable npcs using force absorb
<br>- Created a new cvar "g_allowforceprotect" to enable or disable npcs using force protect
<br>- Created a new cvar "g_allowSaberLocking" to enable or disable saber clashes or locks.
<br>- Modified the force_grasp command to allow to bind to a key and use it as intended.
<br>- NPCs wielding a lightsaber are no longer immune to Force Lightning.</p>

<h3><b><u>UI CHANGES</u></b></h3>
<h3><i><b><u>SETTINGS->SWGL</u></i></b></h3>
<p>- Added the ability to allow/deny NPCs to use any Force Powers not available in the original game.
<br>- Added the ability to allow/deny NPCs to use Force Absorb.
<br>- Added the ability to allow/deny NPCs to use Force Protect.
<br>- Added the ability to enable/disable saber locking.</p>

<h3><i><b><u>CONTROLS->FORCE POWERS</u></i></b></h3>
<p>- Added the ability to bind the 6 new Force Powers to a key.</p>

<h3><b><u>FORCE POWER CHANGES</u></b></h3>

<h3><b><u><i>FORCE STASIS</u></i></b></h3> 
<p>- Mine Crabs are no longer immune and have a 33% chance of being affected.
<br>- All Droid NPC classes are now immune to Force Stasis
<br>- CLASS_HAZARD_TROOPER is now immune to Force Stasis
<br>- Force Stasis rank 2 now has a 10% chance to affect a common Jedi or Sith (decreased from 50%). Powerful Jedi and Sith now have a 1% chance (decreased from 5%).
<br>- Force Stasis rank 3 now has a 50% chance to affect a common Jedi or Sith (decreased from 100%). Powerful Jedi and Sith now have a 5% chance (decreased from 10%).
</p>

<h3><b><u><i>FORCE ABSORB</u></i></b></h3> 
<p>- Level 1 Force Absorb will negate 20% of any incoming Force Blast attack
<br>- Level 2 Force Absorb will negate 60% of any incoming Force Blast attack
<br>- Level 3 Force Absorb will negate all damage of any incoming Force Blast attack.
</p>


<h3><b><u><i>FORCE GRASP</u></i></b></h3> 
<p>- At level 3, there is a 50% chance a common blaster-wielding enemy will drop their weapon.
<br>- Force Grasp can now lift and move around Droid class NPCs.
<br>- Force Grasp's animation was updated to look less like Force Choke.</p>

<h3><b><u><i>FORCE FEAR</u></i></b></h3>
<p>- Corrected a code syntax error allowing Force Fear to work way more often than intended.
<br>- Droids, creatures, Mandalorians, Galak Mech, Hazardtroopers, and npcs using the Concussion Rifle can no longer be affected.
<br>- Powerful Jedi and Sith NPCs are now immune to Force Fear</p>


<h3><b><u>NPC/MODEL CHANGES</u></b></h3>

<h3><b><u><i>REBELLION</u></i></b></h3>
<p>- Changed Ben Kenobi's NPC code from Ben_Kenobi_Unrobed to Ben_Kenobi.</p>

<h3><b><u><i>EMPIRE</u></i></b></h3>
<p>- Updated the Second Sister model to a better quality.
<br>- Updated the Royal Guard model to a better quality, included a robed Shadow Guard variant.
<br>- Improved the Dark Trooper Phase III scaling to be more accurate. Updated the model to a better quality.
<br>- Updated the Phase 2 Dark Trooper with improved lighting and optimization, better rigging, and jetpack flame tags
<br>- Upgraded the shine on Agent Kallus's armor.
<br>- Optimized all Scout Trooper model variants with better LODs and less draw calls (parts)
<br>- Optimized Shock Trooper and Shadow Stormtrooper with LODs
<br>- Ported Jerec's skeleton to the JKA skeleton.</p>

<h3><b><u>MISCELLANEOUS CHANGES</u></b></h3>
<p>- Update Clone Commando Scorch's biography to reflect his appearance in The Bad Batch.</p>
