<h3><u>Patch Notes - 02/07/2022</u></h3>

<h3><b><u>MISSION IMPROVEMENTS</u></b></h3>
<h3><b><u><i>JEDI ACADEMY - ACADEMY 1</u></i></b></h3>
<p>- Added Corran Horn and Kyp Durron among the Jedi Masters.</p>

<h3><b><u><i>JEDI OUTCAST - KEJIM POST</u></i></b></h3>
<p>- Kyle will no longer give lectures about saber styles or telling Jaden that he did well if interacting with a locked door.</p>

<h3><b><u><i>JEDI OUTCAST - KEJIM BASE</u></i></b></h3>
<p>- Mon Mothma and Kyle will now hold the Artusian crystal as intended.</p>

<h3><b><u><i>JEDI OUTCAST - NAR SHADDAA STREETS</u></i></b></h3>
<p>- If attempting to use the Force or perform an inconspicious act before speaking to the Bartender, Kyle will now comment about the importance of keeping a low profile.
<br>- Implemented missing subtitles.
<br>- Kyle will now hand over his bryar pistol instead of a blaster pistol to the cantina bouncers.
<br>- Replaced a blaster pistol item pickup with the bryar pistol as intended.
<br>- The Bartender and Kyle will now hold their respective items instead of invisible objects.</p>

<h3><b><u><i>JEDI OUTCAST - NAR SHADDAA HIDEOUT</u></i></b></h3>
<p>- Implemented missing subtitles.</p>

<h3><b><u><i>JEDI OUTCAST - NAR SHADDAA STARPAD</u></i></b></h3>
<p>- Implemented missing subtitles.</p>

<h3><b><u><i>REVAN VS. MALAK</u></i></b></h3>
<p>- Corrected an issue where the player would spawn with Darth Revan's appearance instead of Jedi Knight Revan's appearance as intended.
<br>- Changed Revan's lightsaber to the correct hilt for the player.

<h3><b><u>NEW CHARACTERS</u></b></h3>
<h3><b><u><i>GALACTIC REPUBLIC</u></i></b></h3>
<p>- Gate Master Jurokk
<br>- Bene
<br>- Whie Malreaux</p>

<h3><b><u>NPC Changes</u></b></h3>

<h3><b><u><i>GALACTIC REPUBLIC</u></i></b></h3>
<p>- Removed Jedi Knights of the Chiss species from the random spawner because IT'S. NOT. CANON!</p>

<h3><b><u><i>SEPARATIST ALLIANCE</u></i></b></h3>
<p>- Updated sounds for General Grievous, removing taunts that made no sense and performances from the 2003 TCW Micro-series.</p>

<h3><b><u><i>REBELLION</u></i></b></h3>
<p>- Increased Kyp Durron's saber defense level from 2 to 3.

<h3><b><u><i>GALACTIC EMPIRE</u></i></b></h3>
<p>- Added new sounds to the Royal Guard</p>

<h3><b><u>FORCE POWER CHANGES</u></b></h3>
<h3><b><u><i>FORCE PUSH</u></i></b></h3>
<p>- NPCs not assigned Force Push can no longer push themselves out of a Force Grip, Grasp, or Drain hold.
<br>- Corrected a bug from the original game where NPCs would lose their Force Push ability after pushing themselves out of a Force Grip, Grasp, or Drain.</p>

<h3><b><u><i>FORCE LIGHTNING</u></i></b></h3>
<p>- Improved the victim's electricity effect to be more accurate depending on the attacker's Force Lightning color.</p>

<h3><b><u><i>SABER THROW</u></i></b></h3>
<p>- Level 3 Saber Throw will no longer lock on to non-combative droids on the Neutral team</p>

<h3><b><u>REMOVED MAPS</u></b></h3>

<p>- Cloud City
<br>- Horizons of Taris
<br>- RC: Republic Cruiser

<br>- Episode I Naboo Hangar

<br>- Episode III Palpatine's Office (Replaced with Fate of the Republic mission map)
<br>- Episode III Palpatine's Rooftop
<br>- Episode IV Mos Eisley Cantina (Interior of the larger Mos Eisley map)
<br>- Sith Vessel
<br>- Atlantica (Not the unlocked map, only the map with locked doors)
<br>- Jungle Temple
<br>- Nowhere
<br>- Serenity (Not the RPG version)
<br>- Space Race
<br>- Imperial Star Destroyer Duel</p>

<h3><b><u>ADDED MAPS</u></b></h3>
<p>- Episode III Galaxies Opera House
<br>- Episode II LiMerge Power Building

<h3><b><u>ANIMATION UPDATES</u></b></h3>
<p>- Lord Vader has been given new stance animations. He will also walk casually when using the Fast, Desann or Tavion styles.
<br>- The Jedi Brute has been given new stance animations.
<br>- The Jedi Temple Guard has been given new stance animations.
<br>- Cin Drallig has been given new stance animations.
<br>- Serra Keto has been given new stance animations.
<br>- Kyle Katarn's Jedi Outcast/Academy appearance will walk casually, mirroring his walking from Jedi Knight II: Jedi Outcast.
<br>- Characters with custom animations will play all intended cutscene animations during the Jedi Academy campaign.
<br>- Added the Jedi Outcast animations to be recognized by the code, fixing multiple issues in Jedi Outcast's cutscenes where characters weren't holding objects or were doing erratic movements.

<h3><b><u>MISCELLANEOUS FIXES</u></b></h3>
<p>- General Grievous's lightsabers have had their damage reduced by 50% in multiplayer.
<br>- Added the Book of Boba Fett main theme as a possible main menu track.
<br>- Corrected Darth Maul (Sith Master) being assigned the incorrect lightsaber in character selection.
<br>- After many years of struggle, Kit Fisto has overcome his helium addiction and will no longer have a high-pitched and erratic voice.</p>
