<li class="PatchNotes">
<h3><u>Patch Notes - 01/14/19</u></h3>

<h3><u><b>MISSION IMPROVEMENTS</u></b></h3>

<h3><b><i><u>REVAN VS. MALAK</b></i></u></h3>
<p>- Revan is no longer a mute, and his dialogue is now fully voiced. Revan's voice was provided by Kevin Smets, the voice behind Logan Starr/Revan in the KOTOR Trilogy.
<br>- Revan's appearance has been changed to his Jedi appearance.
<br>- Revan's initial walk to Malak will no longer stutter and will be smooth.
<br>- Malak will now ignite his lightsaber immediately during his flourish.
<br>- Revan will no longer get distracted by whatever he was looking at before, and will now face toward the oncoming ambush.
<br>- The player will match Revan's viewing angle and will be facing the correct direction in the respective situation.</p>
