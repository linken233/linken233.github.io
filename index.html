  • Patch Notes - 08/29/18

    GENERAL IMPROVEMENTS

    - Multiplayer is now open to all! Knights of the Force Multiplayer will be compatible with almost all pre-existing multiplayer mods.
    - NEW NPC TEAM: SOLO; Any NPC spawned with this team will fend for only themselves and will attack everyone.

    NPC IMPROVEMENTS

    THE GALACTIC REPUBLIC

    - Ahsoka Tano found the right schematics for her lightsabers she used prior to the Galactic Civil War. She is now using the correct, canonical lightsabers.

    THE GALACTIC EMPIRE

    - Juno Eclipse's Rebel NPC variant has been removed in lieu of the NPC Free Choice System.

    BOUNTY HUNTERS

    - Boba Fett stashed away his super-mega-invincible-badass plot armor somewhere on Ord Mantell and forgot to take it with him. Boba Fett will no longer be unkillable except during his campaign mission.

    NEW LIGHTSABERS

    - Ahsoka Tano (Star Wars: Rebels)
    - Darth Maul (Star Wars: Rebels)

    LIGHTSABER CHANGES

    - General Grievous' four armed lightsabers have reduced damage to account for damage balance.
    - The CIS plugged the Magnaguard's electrostaves in the wrong outlet. They will now do reduced damage.

    MULTIPLAYER

    - SFX Lightsabers have been removed from Multiplayer to allow for compatibility with other mods.
    - All KotF weapons have been removed from Multiplayer to allow for compatibility with other mods.
    - RGB Lightsabers have been removed from Multiplayer to allow for compatibility with other mods.
    - The NPC limit in Multiplayer has been increased to allow for the presence of all current npcs developed.

    MISCELLANEOUS CHANGES

    - Sound fixes for certain characters and weapons have been implemented.
