• Patch Notes - 08/12/2019

    HIGHLIGHTS

    NEW MISSION: OPERATION: KNIGHTFALL; Re-visit the event that changed the galaxy forever from Star Wars Episode III: Revenge Of The Sith. Play as the newly appointed Darth Vader, who goes to the Jedi Temple to destroy the treacherous Jedi and bring order to the galaxy. Play as Cin Drallig, who must defend the Temple from the evil that has attempted to desecrate the great Jedi Order. Or play as a Clone Trooper of the 501st Legion and assist Darth Vader in crippling the Jedi and laying waste to all that oppose the great Empire. The choice is yours

    Featuring the voices of (in order of appearance)
    - Linken
    - Darth Voiid
    - Awec

    NEW SABER COLOR: DARKSABER; The Darksaber arrives in Knights of the Force as a fully selectable Saber Color. Choose your prefered weapon, apply the blade, and bring honor to the Mandalorians, Single Player only! (Console command sabercolor 1 darksaber)

    MISSION IMPROVEMENTS

    OPERATION: KNIGHTFALL

    - (ANAKIN'S POV) Fixed an issue where the doors in Stage 3 would seemingly lead to a void in the Force where the entire mission would break.
    - (ANAKIN'S POV) A temple maintenance droid has fixed a door leading to Shaak Ti's meditation chambers. The void in the Force in that location has been contained.
    - (CLONE POV) The Clone has come well-supplied with ammunition clips and bacta injections. The clone will completely heal every 10 seconds if his health is at or below 25%.
    - (CLONE POV) Stage 3 will no longer crash.
    - (CIN'S POV) Players willing to strike down Palpatine can now do so.
    - (CIN'S POV) After weeks of negotiating with Cin Drallig, he finally agreed to actually show up for the 1st stage.

    CHARACTER IMPROVEMENTS

    THE GALACTIC REPUBLIC

    - Chancellor Palpatine decided to stop attempting his ventriloquist act and will now move his mouth when he talks.

    THE GALACTIC EMPIRE

    - Darth Sidious decided to stop attempting his ventriloquist act and will now move his mouth when he talks.

    LIGHTSABER CHANGES

    - The Darksaber has been reworked so that the on/off switch will now work as intended. All lightsaber blades can be used with the Darksaber.
