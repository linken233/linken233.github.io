  • Patch Notes - 05/09/2019

    HIGHLIGHTS

    NEW MISSION: THE TRAYUS CORE; Celebrating 15 years of Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords, experience the final duel on Malachor V as Jedi Exile Meetra Surik confronts Darth Traya, the Lord of Betrayal. Surik's voice was provided by Kathleen Russell.

    MISSION IMPROVEMENTS

    THE TRAYUS CORE

    - A mission briefing will now properly be displayed.
    - Changed preview image of the mission in the bonus mission menu to one of a higher quality.
    - Traya's health in her 1st phase has been increased from 1000 to 2000.
    - Traya can no longer be affected by the Force during the 1st phase.
    - Increased Traya's health in the 2nd phase from 3000 to 9000.
    - Traya can no longer take damage during the 2nd phase while she's firing lightning.
    - Lightsaber health has been increased from 400 to 600.
    - Lightsabers can no longer be affected by the Force.

    NEW MODELS

    THE OLD REPUBLIC

    - Meetra Surik (New head)

    CHARACTER SELECTION

    THE OLD REPUBLIC

    - Meetra Surik (Female), will be using a placeholder icon until a proper icon can be made.

    CHARACTER CHANGES

    THE OLD REPUBLIC

    - Meetra Surik has come to terms with what happened on Malachor V and has completely embraced the Light Side of the Force. Meetra will no longer have access to Force Grip or Force Lightning. Additionally, with this newfound peace, Surik has changed her lightsaber crystal from blue to cyan.
    - New sounds for Meetra Surik.
    - New sounds for Kreia.

    NPC REMOVALS

    THE OLD REPUBLIC

    - Meetra Surik (Dark Side Female, Dark Side Male)

    MISCELLANEOUS CHANGES

    - Meetra's icon in character selection is now changed to her female version (placeholder icon for now).
    - Meetra's title in character selection is now "Meetra Surik" instead of "Exile".
